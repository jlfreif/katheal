## Story

### Spread 1: ma-01.yaml
**Node Type:** Solo | **Beat:** Ordinary world + desire hint

#### Description

Maya paints in her Madrid bedroom, creating bold colorful art, until a shimmer of light appears that doesn't match her paints.

#### Page 5 (Left) - Character in their ordinary world

**Visual:**
Maya sits cross-legged on her bedroom floor surrounded by paint supplies,
her pink-streaked sandy-blond hair catching afternoon light from the
window. Bold colorful paintings cover the walls. She's painting a dance
scene with energetic brushstrokes, tongue poking out in concentration.
Music plays softly in the background.

**Text:**
Maya's brush swept across the paper in bold strokes—bright pink, electric
blue, sunshine yellow. She was painting a dancer mid-leap, all energy
and motion. "That's not right," she muttered, adding more pink. Then
more blue. The painting got louder, bolder, more Maya. Her cousin Elise
would probably paint it gentle and perfect. But Maya liked it loud.

#### Page 6 (Right) - First hint of something different

**Visual:**
Maya freezes, paintbrush hovering, as the painted dancer on her paper
begins to glow with golden light that has nothing to do with her paints.
The glow pulses gently, inviting. Her eyes widen with delighted curiosity.
Pink paint drips forgotten from her brush. The light seems to dance in
rhythm with music only it can hear.

**Text:**
The dancer on her paper started to glow. Maya blinked, but the light
didn't fade. It pulsed softly, goldenly, moving like the figure wanted
to dance right off the page. Her paint couldn't do that. She leaned
closer, brush forgotten. The glow brightened as if responding to her
attention. "Whoa," Maya breathed. "Are you magic?"

### Spread 2: ma-02.yaml
**Node Type:** Solo | **Beat:** Inciting incident

#### Description

Maya touches the glowing dancer and finds herself pulled into Kathaél, where performances and dances are frozen mid-movement.

#### Page 7 (Left) - Discovery of the problem

**Visual:**
Maya stands between worlds, her paint-stained fingers touching a shimmering

**Text:**

Through it she saw a meadow filled with frozen performances—dancers
stuck mid-leap, musicians silent with instruments raised, performers
captured in moments that should have flowed into the next. Golden threads
connected them all, flickering like candles about to die. Something was
very wrong.

#### Page 8 (Right) - Decision to act

**Visual:**
Maya steps fully into Kathaél, standing in the meadow of frozen performances.
Her Madrid bedroom fades to translucent behind her. She spins once,
testing the space, and frozen dancers seem to lean toward her motion.
Golden threads from the Heart Crystal drift closer. Her expression shows
bold determination mixed with excited curiosity.

**Text:**
Maya stepped through, and her bedroom dissolved like watercolor in rain.
The meadow surrounded her, silent where it should have been full of music
and movement. She spun experimentally, and the frozen dancers seemed to
sway toward her, yearning. "You need someone to help you finish," Maya
understood. She grinned, bold and ready. "Perfect. I love a challenge."
