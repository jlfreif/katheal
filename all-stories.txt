KATHAÉL: THE FOUR-CHARACTER STORY WEB

This document contains all four interconnected character stories from the
enchanted realm of Kathaél, where inner courage shapes the world.

Characters:
- Maya (ma): 9 years old, Courage to Be Yourself
- Max (mx): 12 years old, Courage to Keep Going After a Mistake
- Elise (el): 9 years old, Courage to Try Something New
- Noah (no): 12 years old, Courage to Imagine Something Better

Narrative Nodes:
- Spread 3: Maya & Elise meet (cousins reunite)
- Spread 4: Noah & Elise meet (seeking guidance)
- Spread 5: Max & Noah meet (analytical boys)
- Spread 6: Max & Maya mirror (authentic approaches)
- Spread 7: Noah & Elise mirror (parallel struggles)

# Maya's Story

**Character Code:** MA
**Total Spreads:** 12
**Layout:** 2 pages per spread, 1 image per page, 3-4 sentences per scene

## Story

### Spread 1: ma-01.yaml
**Node Type:** Solo | **Beat:** Ordinary world + desire hint

#### Description

Maya paints in her Madrid bedroom, creating bold colorful art, until a shimmer of light appears that doesn't match her paints.

#### Page 5 (Left) - Character in their ordinary world

**Visual:**
Maya sits cross-legged on her bedroom floor surrounded by paint supplies,
her pink-streaked sandy-blond hair catching afternoon light from the
window. Bold colorful paintings cover the walls. She's painting a dance
scene with energetic brushstrokes, tongue poking out in concentration.
Music plays softly in the background.

**Text:**
Maya's brush swept across the paper in bold strokes—bright pink, electric
blue, sunshine yellow. She was painting a dancer mid-leap, all energy
and motion. "That's not right," she muttered, adding more pink. Then
more blue. The painting got louder, bolder, more Maya. Her cousin Elise
would probably paint it gentle and perfect. But Maya liked it loud.

#### Page 6 (Right) - First hint of something different

**Visual:**
Maya freezes, paintbrush hovering, as the painted dancer on her paper
begins to glow with golden light that has nothing to do with her paints.
The glow pulses gently, inviting. Her eyes widen with delighted curiosity.
Pink paint drips forgotten from her brush. The light seems to dance in
rhythm with music only it can hear.

**Text:**
The dancer on her paper started to glow. Maya blinked, but the light
didn't fade. It pulsed softly, goldenly, moving like the figure wanted
to dance right off the page. Her paint couldn't do that. She leaned
closer, brush forgotten. The glow brightened as if responding to her
attention. "Whoa," Maya breathed. "Are you magic?"

### Spread 2: ma-02.yaml
**Node Type:** Solo | **Beat:** Inciting incident

#### Description

Maya touches the glowing dancer and finds herself pulled into Kathaél, where performances and dances are frozen mid-movement.

#### Page 7 (Left) - Discovery of the problem

**Visual:**
Maya stands between worlds, her paint-stained fingers touching a shimmering
portal where her painting transformed into a doorway. Through it, she sees
a meadow with frozen dancers—shadow figures stuck mid-leap, mid-spin,
unable to complete their movements. Golden threads connect them but flicker
weakly. Her athletic build is poised on tiptoes, ready to leap through.

**Text:**
When Maya touched the glowing dancer, the painting opened like a door.
Through it she saw a meadow filled with frozen performances—dancers
stuck mid-leap, musicians silent with instruments raised, performers
captured in moments that should have flowed into the next. Golden threads
connected them all, flickering like candles about to die. Something was
very wrong.

#### Page 8 (Right) - Decision to act

**Visual:**
Maya steps fully into Kathaél, standing in the meadow of frozen performances.
Her Madrid bedroom fades to translucent behind her. She spins once,
testing the space, and frozen dancers seem to lean toward her motion.
Golden threads from the Heart Crystal drift closer. Her expression shows
bold determination mixed with excited curiosity.

**Text:**
Maya stepped through, and her bedroom dissolved like watercolor in rain.
The meadow surrounded her, silent where it should have been full of music
and movement. She spun experimentally, and the frozen dancers seemed to
sway toward her, yearning. "You need someone to help you finish," Maya
understood. She grinned, bold and ready. "Perfect. I love a challenge."

### Spread 3: el-ma-03.yaml (joint with EL)
**Node Type:** Meeting Node | **Beat:** Attempt #1 (simple/obvious)

#### Description

Maya and Elise discover each other in a fading meadow, both trying obvious solutions that don't work. The cousins recognize each other with joy and surprise.

#### Page 9 (Left) - Trying the obvious solution

**Visual:**
Maya leaps and spins in the center of a faded meadow, her movements bold
and energetic. She wears her signature pink-streaked hair flying as she
dances, trying to bring life back through movement. Grey flowers surround

**Text:**

wind itself. If colors were fading, she'd dance them back to life. Her
feet pounded the earth in rhythm, bold and decisive. The flowers barely

the distance, someone knelt among the grey blooms, moving carefully.

#### Page 10 (Right) - Realizing it doesn't work

**Visual:**

their presence.

**Text:**

her painting, eyes going wide. "Maya?!" They stared at each other for
one breathless second. Then both burst out laughing, the sound bright
and startled in the grey meadow. "What are YOU doing here?" they said
at exactly the same time. The threads overhead pulsed warmer, as if
recognizing their connection.

### Spread 5: ma-05.yaml
**Node Type:** Solo | **Beat:** Attempt #3 (bigger world / stakes up)

#### Description

**Text:**

really bad."

#### Page 14 (Right) - Encountering misleading help

**Visual:**

**Text:**

"Technique! Form! You must perform CORRECTLY!" They demonstrated perfect
rigid movements, each gesture sharp and controlled. Maya tried to copy,
but her body wanted to flow and bounce and add her own flair. The frozen
dancers around them seemed to strain harder, as if the rules were choking
them. "This feels wrong," Maya said quietly.

**Visual:**

bright and true.

**Text:**

leaps mixed with silly spins. Her pink-streaked hair flying. Not perfect,
not proper, just...Maya. The shadow-audiences dissolved like smoke. The
compass needle swung toward her and held steady, pointing at her heart.
Flowers bloomed where her feet touched the stage, bright and real and
exactly right.
